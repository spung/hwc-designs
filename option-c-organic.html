What "human-written" actually means.

We know it sounds obvious. In 2025, it isn't. Here's exactly what you're getting when you order from us.

Free-range developers

Our developers work in natural light, take breaks, and form opinions about your architecture. They are not models. They are people. They have children who interrupt calls. We consider this a feature.

Hand-typed, every character

Each line of code passes through a human brain before it touches a keyboard. No tab-completions accepted. No suggestions adopted without review. No ghost-written code in our products.

No AI residue — guaranteed

We test every commit. Our developers work without AI tools enabled. We audit their environments monthly. Zero tolerance policy. Violators are let go. Yes, this has happened.

Full documentation of origin

We provide a chain-of-custody record: who wrote it, when, on what machine, in what mental state (roughly). Your legal team will appreciate this in 2027. Trust us on this one.

Genuinely slow turnaround

We will not compete with AI on speed. A human needs time to think. Our timelines reflect this honestly. If you need code in four hours, we are not your vendor. We say this warmly.

Bugs with a face attached

Every bug in our code has a human responsible for it. Their name is in the git log. You can email them. They will feel bad about it. This is fundamentally different from AI-generated errors.
